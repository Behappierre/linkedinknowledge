## Key Lessons Learned (January 2026)

1. **Paraphrase Penalty:** Never qualify borrowed credibility with "I paraphrase"
2. **Soft Tech Positioning:** When source is potential client, frame platform need as question
3. **Retired Practitioners Are Gold:** They engage freely and validate with historical context
4. **The Toblerone Effect:** Light humor after borrowed credibility opening can work
5. **Comment Threads Drive Distribution:** Quality practitioner debate > reaction count
6. **Network Rail 9% Is Breakthrough:** Technical rail content reaching infrastructure owner
