## 🎯 The Four Success Patterns (February 2026)

### Pattern 1: Industry Critique + Humour (Target: 50K-90K)
- Metric everyone experiences (PPM, delays) + wry observation + named framework + cross-industry question
- Peaks rapidly, sustained growth
- Example: PPM "On Time (ish)" — 87,115 impressions

### Pattern 2: Cross-Sector Borrowed Credibility (Target: 25K-45K)
- Adjacent sector insight + operator quote (no qualifiers) + "I assumed X, but learned Y" + visual
- Example: Copenhagen Airport (42,653), Electric Bus (23,486)

### Pattern 3: Regulatory Analysis with Named Frameworks (Target: 25K-40K, slow-burn)
- RAIB/regulatory finding as opener + named frameworks + prose only + cross-sector question
- Grows 400%+ after Day 1 — "slow-burn viral"
- Example: Shap Landslip (37,726)

### Pattern 4: Named Hero + Systems Insight (Target: 15K-20K)
- Observe frontline excellence, name the individual, connect to coordination failure
- Generates relationship outcomes (EMR leadership recognition)
- Example: James/EMR (16,623)

**All four patterns are wins. Different purposes.**

## 🎓 Key Rules (Updated February 2026)

### What ALWAYS Works
✅ Clean borrowed credibility — quote stands on its own, no qualifiers  
✅ Named frameworks (create vocabulary, own the category)  
✅ Prose only — no bullet points, no numbered lists  
✅ "I assumed X, but learned Y" framing  
✅ Specific numbers and operational details  
✅ Real photos (not AI-generated)  
✅ Strategic engagement questions  
✅ Cross-industry orchestration angle  
✅ Named individuals with their title and company  

### What NEVER Works
❌ Qualifying borrowed credibility ("to paraphrase", "not the exact word but...")  
❌ Bullet points in post body (26x impression penalty — proven)  
❌ Corporate amplification / repost content  
❌ Posts without engagement hooks  
❌ Buzzwords without concrete examples  
❌ Generic stock photos or AI-generated images  
❌ More than 4 hashtags  
❌ Posting Friday afternoon or weekends
