## 📐 The Structural Rules (Non-Negotiable)

Four penalties are now proven with data. Violating any kills distribution:

**❌ Never qualify borrowed credibility** — "Not the exact word but I paraphrase" caused a 23x impression reduction (Groundhog Day: 1,088 vs comparable posts averaging 35,000+).

**❌ Never use bullet points in post body** — Audenshaw RAIB post used bullets: 1,446 impressions. Shap Landslip, same topic, prose only: 37,765 impressions. **26x penalty**.

**❌ Never post on Sunday** — Underground/Solar (Sunday, March 2026): 855 impressions despite 64.1% seniority and a live news hook. No distribution window.

**❌ Never post twice on the same day** — EU Telematics + Heathrow (Feb 24, 2026): both capped at 6-7K. Estimated combined loss: 20K-25K impressions.

## 🎯 The Five Success Patterns (March 2026)

### Pattern 1: Industry Critique + Humour (Target: 50K-90K)
Metric everyone experiences + wry observation + named framework + cross-industry question.
Example: PPM "On Time (ish)" — 87,246 impressions

### Pattern 2: RAIB Named Framework (Target: 35K-55K)
RAIB Safety Digest opener + clinical framework definition + cross-industry transferability.
Example: Helpston/Override Drift — 50,484 | Shap — 37,765

### Pattern 3: Cross-Sector Borrowed Credibility (Target: 25K-45K)
Adjacent sector insight + operator quote (no qualifiers) + "I assumed X, but learned Y" + visual.
Example: Copenhagen Airport (42,905), Electric Bus (23,515)

### Pattern 4: Policy + Multi-Source Framework (Target: 15K-25K)
Minister-level quote + named layers + digital layer framing + rail industry engagement question.
Example: George Bradshaw Address (20,983)

### Pattern 5: Named Hero + Systems Insight (Target: 15K-20K)
Observe frontline excellence, name the individual, pivot to coordination failure.
Example: James/EMR (17,658 — record 178 reactions; triggered formal staff recognition)

## 🎓 Key Rules (March 2026)

### What ALWAYS Works
✅ Clean borrowed credibility — quote stands on its own, no qualifiers
✅ Named frameworks (create vocabulary, own the category)
✅ Prose only — no bullet points, no numbered lists
✅ "I assumed X, but learned Y" framing
✅ Specific numbers and operational details
✅ Real photos (not AI-generated)
✅ Strategic engagement questions
✅ Cross-industry orchestration angle
✅ Named individuals with their title and company
✅ "The technology worked" inversion for safety content (leads with success, then identifies the gap)

### What NEVER Works
❌ Qualifying borrowed credibility ("to paraphrase", "not the exact word but...") — 23x penalty
❌ Bullet points in post body — 26x penalty
❌ Posting on Sunday — no distribution window regardless of content quality
❌ Two posts on the same day — algorithm split, 20K+ combined impressions lost
❌ Precision content on Friday — confirmed ceiling
❌ Corporate amplification / repost content
❌ Posts without engagement hooks
❌ Generic stock photos or AI-generated images
❌ More than 4 hashtags
