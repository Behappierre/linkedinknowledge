## 🚨 CRITICAL FINDINGS: Structural Failure Patterns

### The Paraphrase Penalty (PROVEN)

**Posts with clean borrowed credibility:**
- PPM: "I'm fine with five minutes" → 87,115 impressions
- Copenhagen: Operations Director quote (no qualifier) → 42,653 impressions
- Railway Within Railway: "A Railway within a Railway" → 16,319 impressions
- Electric Bus: "A missed charge at 2pm..." → 23,486 impressions
- **Average: 42,393 impressions**

**Post with qualified borrowed credibility:**
- Groundhog Day: "Not the exact word but I paraphrase" → 1,850 impressions
- **Penalty: 23x impression reduction**

### The Bullet Point Penalty (PROVEN)

**Post with bullet points in body:**
- Audenshaw: Used "- Structures saw the bridge. / - Track saw the rail. / - The warnings never combined..." → 1,446 impressions
- Correct topic (RAIB). Correct format (analysis). Strong orchestration insight. Structural failure only.

**Post without bullet points, similar RAIB topic:**
- Shap: Prose throughout → 37,726 impressions
- **Penalty: 26x impression reduction**

**Combined rule:** Never qualify borrowed credibility. Never use bullet points in posts. Both penalties are severe and documented.
