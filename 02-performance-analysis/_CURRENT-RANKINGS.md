## ❌ What NEVER Works

### 1. Qualified Borrowed Credibility
**Evidence:**
- "Not the exact word but I paraphrase" → 1,836 impressions
- Clean borrowed credibility average → 34,121 impressions
- **Penalty: 18x-46x reduction**

**Never do:**
- "Not the exact word but I paraphrase"
- "Something along the lines of"
- "If I recall correctly"
- Any qualifier suggesting approximation

**Always do:**
- Direct quote in quotation marks
- Or anonymize completely ("An operator told me")
- Never acknowledge uncertainty in attribution

---

### 2. Reposts (Even With Added Value)
**Evidence:**
- Tom Bailey (with framing): 854 impressions
- Belgium ETCS (with UK comparison): 459 impressions
- Energy recruitment (with orchestration angle): 342 impressions

**Algorithm treats ALL reposts as promotional content**

**Never repost:**
- Colleague success stories
- Company announcements
- Industry news articles
- Even with your own commentary

---

### 3. Posts Without Engagement Hooks
**Evidence:**
- Trade Show: 1,346 impressions, 0 comments
- Good content, specific stats, but no invitation to respond

**Always end with:**
- Strategic question
- Comparison invitation
- Provocative challenge
- Experience solicitation
