## Lessons Learned

### Lesson 1: Always End with Engagement Question
**What happened:** Post ended with "A compelling vision for the future" (statement)
**Result:** Only 2 comments despite 32 reactions
**Fix:** Should have ended with: "What's your organization's equivalent of 'learning to dance'—which teams need better choreography?"

### Lesson 2: Conference Posts Need Engagement Seeds
**What happened:** Only Steve White commented (1 of 3 tagged executives)
**Why:** Others may not have seen it, or didn't feel invited to engage
**Fix:** DM the tagged executives within 2 hours of posting: "Just posted about your insight from the conference - would love your perspective if you have a moment"

### Lesson 3: "Note in Three Words" Shows You're Not Just Parachuting In
**What worked:** Observing Southeastern → South Eastern Railway rebrand detail
**Why it matters:** Demonstrates you're embedded in industry, not consultant tourist
**Apply to future:** Always include one industry insider observation that shows you understand the context

### Lesson 4: Conference Content is Time-Sensitive
**Evidence:** 0 saves (not reference material), moderate impressions
**Why:** Conference insights feel ephemeral ("you had to be there")
**Solution:** Frame conference insights as broader industry trends, not just event reports

## Future Conference Content Recommendations

### Before Next Conference:
1. **Plan engagement strategy** - Who to @ tag, who to DM after posting
2. **Prepare engagement question** - Draft before event
3. **Take notes on quotable moments** - Capture exact phrases
4. **Photograph speakers** - Get permission if possible
5. **Connect with speakers afterward** - DM within 24h of event

### During Conference:
1. **Listen for visceral, specific insights** (like "learning to dance")
2. **Note who said what** (for attribution)
3. **Observe industry details** (like "three words" rebrand)
4. **Take photos for visual proof** you were there
5. **Ask speakers permission** to quote them on LinkedIn

### After Conference:
1. **Post within 48 hours** while still timely
2. **@ tag speakers** in body text, not just at end
3. **DM tagged people** if no engagement after 1-2 hours
4. **End with engagement question** always
5. **Follow up with commenters** to build relationships
