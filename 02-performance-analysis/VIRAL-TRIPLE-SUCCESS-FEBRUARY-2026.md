## 🚨 The Paraphrase Penalty - CRITICAL LESSON

### The Evidence

**Posts with CLEAN borrowed credibility:**
- PPM: "I'm fine with five minutes" → **84,022 impressions**
- Copenhagen: Operations Director quote → **40,089 impressions**
- Railway: "A Railway within a Railway" → **16,140 impressions**
- BEMU: "A missed charge at 2pm..." → **6,882 impressions**

**Average: 36,783 impressions**

---

**Post with QUALIFIED borrowed credibility:**
- Groundhog Day: "Not the exact word but I paraphrase" → **1,836 impressions**

**The Paraphrase Penalty: 20x-40x impression reduction**

### What This Means

When you qualify borrowed credibility with phrases like:
- "Not the exact word but I paraphrase"
- "Something along the lines of"
- "If I recall correctly"

You destroy authenticity and kill algorithmic distribution.
