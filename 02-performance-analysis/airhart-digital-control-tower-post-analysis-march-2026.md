## 12. Lessons Learned

**The self-comment can outperform the post opener.** "A 12-minute signal failure cascades into a three-hour, multi-million pound disruption" is more visceral than the Echave C-suite validation quote that opened the main post. Before finalising any post opener, scan all available failure scenarios and operational specifics — the strongest hook is often buried in the details, not the executive quote.

**Aviation channel presence does not guarantee aviation channel dominance.** Post 19 opened the channel at 7.2%. Post 20 showed 5.5% at 8h but the confirmed final lost it from the top 5. A single aviation-named post creates a temporary channel; sustained aviation content is required to hold it at 5%+.

**ROSCOs can be reached via knowledge cliff framing.** Porterbrook viewer on a post about 8,000 specialists retiring is not coincidental. The workforce transition challenge is as acute for rolling stock lessors as for operators. This is a new angle for content targeting.

**NR doubles between 8h and final on GBR-adjacent content.** 1.9% at 8h to 4.5% at confirmed final is a pattern: the NR community circulates content slowly, via internal networks and DMs. GBR content peaks later than the initial distribution.
